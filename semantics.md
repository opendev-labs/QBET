### Semantics.md

#### Core Semantics
- **Qubit**: `qubit(value)`
- **Matrix**: `matrix(values)`
- **Karma**: `karma(action)`
- **Dharma**: `dharma(purpose)`
- **Balance**: `balance(state1, state2)`

#### Cosmic Semantics
- **Earth**: `earth(scope)`
- **Mars**: `mars(scope)`
- **Sun**: `sun(core_function)`
- **Milky Way**: `milky_way(system)`
- **Orion**: `orion(constellation)`

#### Subatomic Semantics
- **Electron**: `electron(charge)`
- **Proton**: `proton(charge)`
- **Neutron**: `neutron(charge)`
- **Quark**: `quark(type)`

#### Quantum Semantics
- **Wave**: `wave(interference, superposition)`
- **Particle**: `particle(state, collision)`
